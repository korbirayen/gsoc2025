Slogan: Connect. Share. Discover.

Tech Stack: python, javascript, kubernetes, reactjs, terraform

Topic: web, health, data, infrastructure, software

Description: Connect. Share. Discover. Data for the Common Good is dedicated to building communities, platforms, and ecosystems that maximize the potential of data to drive discovery and improve human health. Headquartered in the Department of Pediatrics at the University of Chicago, our team of experts works with collaborators all over the world to connect and share useful, high-quality data between institutions, groups, and countries to increase opportunities for discovery.
