MIT App Inventor

Slogan: Anyone can build apps with global impact

Tech Stack: javascript, java, gwt, swift

Topic: education, development tools, android, ios

Description: Anyone can build apps with global impact MIT App Inventor is a free, open source web app that anyone can use to build mobile apps. It has been used by over 8 million people worldwide who have built more than 30 million apps. It is available in twelve different languages and used by people from ages 13 and up.
